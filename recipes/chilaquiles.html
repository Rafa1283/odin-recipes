Chilaquiles

Description

The most iconic dish for a hangover in Mexico is now at your disposal. Feel like a real mexican while you enjoy the spicy flavor, the crunchyness, and the mixture of amazing, yet simple flavors. Don't let this awful hangover prevent you for enjoying life, let's get to it:

Ingredients

  • Tortilla chips
  • Salsa verde
  • 2 eggs
  • Chopped onions
  • Cilantro
  • Cream and “quesillo”

Steps

  1. In a plate, put some tortilla chips as is
  2. Prepare the two eggs like you want, either scrambled or fried
  3. Heat the salsa verde and pour it on top of the tortilla chips
  4. Put the eggs on top and add some cream, chopped onions, cilantro and “quesillo” in the mixture
  5. Enjoy, bitch
